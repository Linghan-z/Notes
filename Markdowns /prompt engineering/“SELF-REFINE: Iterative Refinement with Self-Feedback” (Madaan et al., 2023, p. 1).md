<span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FPHI2BBHP%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%222%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22pageIndex%22%3A1%2C%22rects%22%3A%5B%5B154.718%2C426.119%2C211.571%2C435.026%5D%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open-pdf/library/items/PHI2BBHP?page=2">“<span style="background-color: #ff666680">SELF-REFINE</span>”</a></span> <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/FSH77WHR">Madaan et al., 2023, p. 2</a></span>)</span>

*   在

    <span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FPHI2BBHP%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%222%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22pageIndex%22%3A1%2C%22rects%22%3A%5B%5B155.524%2C415.21%2C201.35%2C424.117%5D%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open-pdf/library/items/PHI2BBHP?page=2">“<span style="background-color: #ff666680">FEEDBACK</span>”</a></span>

*   步骤：

    *   <span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FPHI2BBHP%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%222%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22rects%22%3A%5B%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open-pdf/library/items/PHI2BBHP?page=NaN"><span style="background-color: #ffd40080">“Given an initial output generated by a model M, we pass it back to the same model M to get </span><strong><em><span style="background-color: #ffd40080">feedback</span></em></strong><span style="background-color: #ffd40080">”</span></a></span>

        <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/FSH77WHR">Madaan et al., 2023, p. 2</a></span>)</span>

        给定模型 M 生成的初始输出，我们将其传递回同一模型 M 以获得反馈

    *   <span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FPHI2BBHP%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%222%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22rects%22%3A%5B%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open-pdf/library/items/PHI2BBHP?page=NaN"><span style="background-color: #ffd40080">“Then, the feedback is passed back to the same model to </span><strong><em><span style="background-color: #ffd40080">refine</span></em></strong><span style="background-color: #ffd40080"> the previously-generated draft”</span></a></span>

        <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/FSH77WHR">Madaan et al., 2023, p. 2</a></span>)</span>

        然后，反馈被传递回同一模型以完善先前生成的草稿

    *   <span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FPHI2BBHP%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%222%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22rects%22%3A%5B%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open-pdf/library/items/PHI2BBHP?page=NaN"><span style="background-color: #ffd40080">“This process is repeated either for a specified number of iterations or until M determines that no further refinement is necessary”</span></a></span>

        <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/FSH77WHR">Madaan et al., 2023, p. 2</a></span>)</span>

        重复此过程指定的迭代次数或直到 M 确定不需要进一步细化

    *   <span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FPHI2BBHP%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%222%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22rects%22%3A%5B%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open-pdf/library/items/PHI2BBHP?page=NaN"><span style="background-color: #ffd40080">“We use few-shot prompting (Brown et al., 2020) to guide M to both generate feedback and incorporate the feedback into an improved draft”</span></a></span>

        <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/FSH77WHR">Madaan et al., 2023, p. 2</a></span>)</span>

### 文章中的实验：

<span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FPHI2BBHP%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%222%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22rects%22%3A%5B%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open-pdf/library/items/PHI2BBHP?page=NaN">“7 generation tasks that span diverse domains, including natural language and source-code generation.”</a></span> <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/FSH77WHR">Madaan et al., 2023, p. 2</a></span>)</span> 7 跨越不同领域的生成任务，包括自然语言和源代码生成。

### 结论：

<span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FPHI2BBHP%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%222%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22rects%22%3A%5B%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open-pdf/library/items/PHI2BBHP?page=NaN">“<span style="background-color: #ff666680">In essence, our results show that even when an LLM cannot generate an optimal output on its first try, the LLM can often provide useful feedback and improve its own output accordingly.</span>”</a></span> <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/FSH77WHR">Madaan et al., 2023, p. 2</a></span>)</span> 从本质上讲，我们的结果表明，即使LLM在第一次尝试时无法产生最佳输出，LLM通常也可以提供有用的反馈并相应地改进其自身的输出。

<span class="highlight" data-annotation="%7B%22attachmentURI%22%3A%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FPHI2BBHP%22%2C%22pageLabel%22%3A%222%22%2C%22position%22%3A%7B%22rects%22%3A%5B%5D%7D%2C%22citationItem%22%3A%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%7D" ztype="zhighlight"><a href="zotero://open-pdf/library/items/PHI2BBHP?page=NaN">“In turn, SELF-REFINE provides an effective way to <span style="background-color: #ff666680">obtain better outputs from a single model without any additional training</span>, via iterative (self-)feedback and refinement.”</a></span> <span class="citation" data-citation="%7B%22citationItems%22%3A%5B%7B%22uris%22%3A%5B%22http%3A%2F%2Fzotero.org%2Fusers%2F10290592%2Fitems%2FFSH77WHR%22%5D%2C%22locator%22%3A%222%22%7D%5D%2C%22properties%22%3A%7B%7D%7D" ztype="zcitation">(<span class="citation-item"><a href="zotero://select/library/items/FSH77WHR">Madaan et al., 2023, p. 2</a></span>)</span> 反过来，SELF-REFINE 提供了一种有效的方法，通过迭代（自）反馈和细化，无需任何额外的训练即可从单个模型获得更好的输出
